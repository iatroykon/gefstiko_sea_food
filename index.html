Our Story

A family taverna,
by the harbour.

Gefstiko has stood directly across from the main harbour of Limenas since 2005, where the fishing boats come in and the waterfront comes alive at dusk. Traditional at heart and warm in spirit, it's the kind of table you settle into for hours.

There are no menus of mystery here. What's fresh is laid out on the ice, and what you pick is what lands on your plate — sea bream, red mullet, octopus, calamari, sea bass. No middlemen, no guesswork. Just fish, olive oil, lemon and fire.

The Setting

Harbour views.

Outdoor seating that looks straight onto the water, live music drifting across the terrace on some nights, and a deeply comfortable table — noticeably free of the wasps that trouble the spots next door. Just the sea breeze and your plate.

The Ritual

Pick your catch.

An open counter packed with raw ice sits at the heart of the taverna. You walk up, you point, and you choose the exact fish that lands on your table. This is the whole idea.

Meet the ice
